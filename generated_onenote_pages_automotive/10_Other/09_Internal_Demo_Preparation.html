Internal Demo Preparation

Lists preparation steps for internal demos of HMI features, cloud services, diagnostics, and telemetry dashboards.

Reference Note

Internal Demo Preparation is a shared reference for teams that need the same wording, decision, or convention. This page lists preparation steps for internal demos of HMI features, cloud services, diagnostics, and telemetry dashboards.

It should be short enough to stay readable but specific enough that two teams do not interpret the same term or rule differently. The owner should keep examples current because examples are usually what people copy.

Maintained By

Owner teamProduct Engineering
Audienceall technical employees
Review rhythmReview quarterly or when the owner team changes process.
Primary systemsDemo Environment, Telemetry Dashboard, Jira

How Teams Use It

Teams usually open this page while preparing a review, naming something new, writing a decision record, or checking an audit detail. The useful material is demo script, data set, fallback plan.

When the reference affects repositories, artifacts, or architecture records, update Demo Environment, Telemetry Dashboard, and Jira in the same change window.

Useful Material

  • Demo script maintained by Product Engineering.
  • Data set available before work starts.
  • Fallback plan linked from the ticket, request, or workspace.
  • Current owner, review date, linked decision, and affected teams.

Review Habit

When this reference changes, link the decision or review note in Demo Environment and tell the teams that depend on Telemetry Dashboard. If the change affects artifacts, also update Jira.

Checks

  • Owner team is Product Engineering and has accepted the request or review.
  • Required artifacts are available: demo script, data set, fallback plan.
  • Relevant systems are checked: Demo Environment, Telemetry Dashboard, Jira.
  • The final decision or completion note is attached to the ticket, request, or record.
