Meeting Notes Standard

Defines the expected format for project, release, incident, and supplier meeting notes.

Reference Note

Meeting Notes Standard is a shared reference for teams that need the same wording, decision, or convention. This page defines the expected format for project, release, incident, and supplier meeting notes.

It should be short enough to stay readable but specific enough that two teams do not interpret the same term or rule differently. The owner should keep examples current because examples are usually what people copy.

Maintained By

Owner teamEngineering Operations
Audienceall technical employees
Review rhythmReview quarterly or when the owner team changes process.
Primary systemsConfluence, Jira, Calendar

How Teams Use It

Teams usually open this page while preparing a review, naming something new, writing a decision record, or checking an audit detail. The useful material is decision log, action item, owner.

When the reference affects repositories, artifacts, or architecture records, update Confluence, Jira, and Calendar in the same change window.

Useful Material

  • Decision log maintained by Engineering Operations.
  • Action item available before work starts.
  • Owner linked from the ticket, request, or workspace.
  • Current owner, review date, linked decision, and affected teams.

Review Habit

When this reference changes, link the decision or review note in Confluence and tell the teams that depend on Jira. If the change affects artifacts, also update Calendar.

Checks

  • Owner team is Engineering Operations and has accepted the request or review.
  • Required artifacts are available: decision log, action item, owner.
  • Relevant systems are checked: Confluence, Jira, Calendar.
  • The final decision or completion note is attached to the ticket, request, or record.
