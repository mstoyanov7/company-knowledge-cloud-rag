Vendor Evaluation Checklist

Defines evaluation criteria for tools, suppliers, hosted services, and engineering contractors.

Reference Note

Vendor Evaluation Checklist is a shared reference for teams that need the same wording, decision, or convention. This page defines evaluation criteria for tools, suppliers, hosted services, and engineering contractors.

It should be short enough to stay readable but specific enough that two teams do not interpret the same term or rule differently. The owner should keep examples current because examples are usually what people copy.

How Teams Use It

Teams usually open this page while preparing a review, naming something new, writing a decision record, or checking an audit detail. The useful material is evaluation scorecard, risk review, approval decision.

When the reference affects repositories, artifacts, or architecture records, update Procurement Portal, Security Review, and Legal Intake in the same change window.

Review Habit

When this reference changes, link the decision or review note in Procurement Portal and tell the teams that depend on Security Review. If the change affects artifacts, also update Legal Intake.

Checks

  • Owner team is Supplier Integration and has accepted the request or review.
  • Required artifacts are available: evaluation scorecard, risk review, approval decision.
  • Relevant systems are checked: Procurement Portal, Security Review, Legal Intake.
  • The final decision or completion note is attached to the ticket, request, or record.
