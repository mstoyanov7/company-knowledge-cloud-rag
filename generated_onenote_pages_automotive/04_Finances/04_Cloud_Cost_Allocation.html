Finance Note

Cloud Cost Allocation is a finance reference for engineering work where cost, approval, or supplier timing matters. This page documents tag requirements and monthly review process for cloud costs consumed by connected vehicle platforms.

Treat the page like an approval memo. It should make the business reason, cost center, approver, and evidence clear enough that Finance Operations can understand the request without asking the engineering team to rewrite it.

Approval Owner

Owner teamCloud FinOps
Audienceengineering managers, project owners, finance partners
Review rhythmReview quarterly and after policy or vendor changes.
Primary systemsCloud Console, FinOps Dashboard, ERP

Approval Route

The request usually starts in Cloud Console with cloud tag. The approver checks cost report, then the requester attaches service owner before the purchase, invoice, or reimbursement is processed.

If the work is tied to a vehicle program, supplier delivery, or launch support, keep the project reference visible. It helps finance separate one-off team purchases from program cost.

Record Keeping

The expected record contains approval record, cost center, receipt or quote, purchase or invoice ID. Keep it attached to the purchase, supplier, travel, or cost-center record so engineering and finance do not have to reconstruct the decision later.

Finance Checks

  • Owner team is Cloud FinOps and has accepted the request or review.
  • Required artifacts are available: cloud tag, cost report, service owner.
  • Relevant systems are checked: Cloud Console, FinOps Dashboard, ERP.
  • The final decision or completion note is attached to the ticket, request, or record.

Common Delays

  • Work should not begin on supplier or purchase activity before approval is recorded.
  • Wrong cost center mapping can delay invoice handling and reporting.
  • Quotes, receipts, and approval notes should stay attached to the finance record.
