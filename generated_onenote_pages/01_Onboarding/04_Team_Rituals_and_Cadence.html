Team Rituals and Cadence

Recurring meetings and planning cycles a new hire should expect.

Purpose

Team Rituals and Cadence is maintained by Engineering Operations for new hires, managers, onboarding buddies. This onboarding guide recurring meetings and planning cycles a new hire should expect.

Key Facts

  • Teams run two-week sprints starting on Wednesdays.
  • Daily standup is 15 minutes at 10:00 in the team channel huddle.
  • Sprint review and retro happen on the second Tuesday afternoon.
  • Incident reviews are blameless and scheduled within 3 business days of resolution.

Onboarding Steps

  1. Add the team ceremony calendar to your schedule.
  2. Post your standup update before 10:00 if you cannot attend live.
  3. Bring one improvement idea to the retro.
