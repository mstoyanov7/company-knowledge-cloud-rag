First Week Plan for New Engineers

What a new software engineer should accomplish in the first five working days.

Context

First Week Plan for New Engineers is maintained by Engineering Operations for new hires, managers, onboarding buddies. This onboarding guide what a new software engineer should accomplish in the first five working days.

Onboarding Steps

  1. Collect the laptop and sign the asset handover form from IT.
  2. Activate SSO and enroll a second MFA factor before opening any tool.
  3. Clone the sandbox repository and run the bootstrap script.
  4. Open the first starter ticket tagged 'good-first-issue' with your buddy.
  5. Complete security awareness training and confirm to your manager.

Key Facts

  • Day 1 is laptop handover and account activation; accounts are provisioned within 4 business hours of the IT ticket.
  • Every new engineer is paired with an onboarding buddy for the first 30 days.
  • By end of week one the new hire ships one small change to the internal sandbox repository.
  • Mandatory security awareness training must be completed within the first 5 days.
  • The onboarding buddy books a 30-minute daily check-in for the first week.
