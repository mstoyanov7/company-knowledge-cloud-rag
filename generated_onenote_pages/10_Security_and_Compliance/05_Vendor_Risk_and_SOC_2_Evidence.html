Vendor Risk and SOC 2 Evidence

How vendor reviews and audit evidence are organized.

Overview

Vendor Risk and SOC 2 Evidence is maintained by Compliance for all employees, engineers, auditors. This security policy how vendor reviews and audit evidence are organized.

Ownership

Owner teamCompliance
Audienceall employees, engineers, auditors
SystemsCompliance Portal, Document Library

Key Facts

  • New vendors handling customer data must provide a current SOC 2 Type II report.
  • Evidence for our own SOC 2 audit is collected continuously, not at year end.
  • Each control has a named owner and a quarterly evidence refresh.
  • Auditor access is read-only and time-boxed to the audit window.

Control Process

  1. Request the vendor's SOC 2 report.
  2. File it in the vendor risk register.
  3. Refresh control evidence each quarter.

What To Keep

Keep the control ID, review record, evidence link with the related ticket or record.
