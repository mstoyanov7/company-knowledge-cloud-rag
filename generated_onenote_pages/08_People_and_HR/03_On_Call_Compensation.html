On-Call Compensation

How engineers are compensated for on-call duty.

Overview

On-Call Compensation is maintained by People Operations for employees, managers, HR partners. This HR policy how engineers are compensated for on-call duty.

Ownership

Owner teamPeople Operations
Audienceemployees, managers, HR partners
SystemsPagerDuty, HR Portal, Payroll System

Key Facts

  • On-call is one week at a time and rotates so no one does more than one week per month.
  • On-call carries a flat weekly stipend plus time off in lieu for night pages.
  • Being paged after midnight grants a late start the next day.
  • On-call eligibility starts only after on-call shadowing is complete.

Employee Process

  1. Confirm the rotation in the schedule.
  2. Record any night pages for time in lieu.
  3. Hand off cleanly at the end of the week.

What To Keep

Keep the policy source, request record, approval with the related ticket or record.
