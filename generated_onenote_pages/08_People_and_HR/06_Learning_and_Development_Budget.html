Learning and Development Budget

Using the annual training budget.

Context

Learning and Development Budget is maintained by Learning and Development for employees, managers, HR partners. This HR policy using the annual training budget.

Owner and Systems

Owner
Learning and Development
For
employees, managers, HR partners
Systems
HR Portal, Learning Platform, Expense Tool

Employee Process

  1. Pick an eligible course or conference.
  2. Get manager approval in the HR Portal.
  3. Submit the receipt for reimbursement.

Key Facts

  • Each engineer has an annual learning budget of 1,500 EUR.
  • Conferences, courses, books, and certifications are eligible.
  • Manager approval is required before booking; reimbursement needs a receipt.
  • Unused budget does not carry over to the next year.

Systems Involved

This work touches HR Portal, Learning Platform, Expense Tool.
