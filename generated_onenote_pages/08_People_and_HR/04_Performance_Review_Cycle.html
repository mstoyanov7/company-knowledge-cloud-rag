Performance Review Cycle

Timing and inputs for performance reviews.

Summary

Performance Review Cycle is maintained by People Operations for employees, managers, HR partners. This HR policy timing and inputs for performance reviews.

Key Facts

  • Reviews run twice a year, in June and December.
  • Each review includes a self-assessment and at least two peer reviews.
  • Calibration happens at the department level before ratings are shared.
  • Promotion cases are submitted during the December cycle.

Employee Process

  1. Write your self-assessment by the deadline.
  2. Nominate peer reviewers.
  3. Discuss outcomes in your 1:1.
