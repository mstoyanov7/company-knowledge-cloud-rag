Team Budget and Forecast

How engineering managers track their budget.

Overview

Team Budget and Forecast is maintained by Program Finance for managers, project owners, finance partners. This finance process how engineering managers track their budget.

Ownership

Owner teamProgram Finance
Audiencemanagers, project owners, finance partners
SystemsERP, Planning Tool, Finance Dashboard

Key Facts

  • Budgets are set annually and reviewed each quarter.
  • Headcount is the largest line and is forecast in the Planning Tool.
  • A variance over 10% against plan requires a written explanation.
  • Capital purchases are depreciated and tracked separately from operating cost.

Approval Process

  1. Review the monthly actuals against plan.
  2. Update the forecast in the Planning Tool.
  3. Explain variances over 10%.

What To Keep

Keep the approval record, cost center, receipt or quote with the related ticket or record.
