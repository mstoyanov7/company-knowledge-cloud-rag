Vendor Invoice Processing

How supplier invoices are received and paid.

Context

Vendor Invoice Processing is the finance process maintained by Accounts Payable for managers, project owners, finance partners. It covers how supplier invoices are received and paid. Invoices are emailed to invoices@northwind.local or uploaded to the Invoice Portal.

Owner and Systems

Owner
Accounts Payable
For
managers, project owners, finance partners
Systems
Invoice Portal, ERP, Procurement Portal

Approval Process

  1. Match the invoice to its purchase order.
  2. Confirm goods or services were received.
  3. Approve for payment or raise a dispute.

Key Facts

  • Invoices are emailed to invoices@northwind.local or uploaded to the Invoice Portal.
  • An invoice must match an approved purchase order to be paid.
  • Standard payment terms are net 30 from invoice date.
  • Disputed invoices are put on hold and the supplier is notified within 5 days.

Systems Involved

This work touches Invoice Portal, ERP, Procurement Portal.
