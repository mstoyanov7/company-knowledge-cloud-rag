Cloud Cost Management

How teams stay accountable for cloud spend.

Purpose

Cloud Cost Management is the finance process maintained by Cloud FinOps for managers, project owners, finance partners. It covers how teams stay accountable for cloud spend. Every cloud resource must carry team, service, and environment tags.

Key Facts

  • Every cloud resource must carry team, service, and environment tags.
  • Untagged resources are flagged daily and may be stopped after 7 days.
  • Each team gets a monthly cost report and a budget alert at 80% of plan.
  • Non-production environments scale down outside working hours.

Approval Process

  1. Tag all resources at creation.
  2. Review the monthly cost report with your team.
  3. Investigate any spend anomaly alert.
