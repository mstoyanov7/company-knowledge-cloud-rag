Expense Reimbursement

How to claim work-related expenses.

Overview

Expense Reimbursement is maintained by Finance Operations for managers, project owners, finance partners. This finance process how to claim work-related expenses.

Ownership

Owner teamFinance Operations
Audiencemanagers, project owners, finance partners
SystemsExpense Tool, Finance Approval, Payroll System

Key Facts

  • Expenses are submitted in the Expense Tool within 60 days of the purchase.
  • Receipts are mandatory for any expense over 25 EUR.
  • Approved expenses are paid with the next monthly payroll run.
  • Alcohol and personal items are not reimbursable.

Approval Process

  1. Photograph the receipt and create an expense.
  2. Assign the correct cost center.
  3. Submit for manager approval.
