Jira Projects and Boards

Finding the right board and requesting access.

Purpose

Jira Projects and Boards is the tool access guide maintained by Engineering Operations for engineers and team leads. It covers finding the right board and requesting access. Each team has a Jira project keyed by a short code, e.g. NW for the platform team.

Key Facts

  • Each team has a Jira project keyed by a short code, e.g. NW for the platform team.
  • Tickets follow the workflow Backlog, In Progress, In Review, Done.
  • Access is requested through the Access Portal and approved by the project lead.
  • Cross-team work uses linked issues rather than moving tickets between projects.

Request Flow

  1. Request access to the project in the Access Portal.
  2. Use the team board filter for your sprint.
  3. Link cross-team dependencies instead of copying tickets.
