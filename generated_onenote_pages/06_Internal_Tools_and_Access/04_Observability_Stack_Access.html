Observability Stack Access

Getting into Grafana, logs, and traces.

Summary

Observability Stack Access is maintained by Platform Team for engineers and team leads. This tool access guide getting into Grafana, logs, and traces.

Key Facts

  • Dashboards are in Grafana at grafana.northwind.local with SSO.
  • Logs are in Loki and queried through Grafana Explore.
  • Traces are in Tempo, linked from each service dashboard.
  • Alert routing is configured per team in the Alerting section.

Request Flow

  1. Sign in to Grafana with SSO.
  2. Find your team folder of dashboards.
  3. Use Explore for ad-hoc log and trace queries.

What To Keep

Keep the access ticket, owner approval, role mapping with the related ticket or record.
