Email and Distribution Lists

Requesting shared mailboxes and distribution lists.

Context

Email and Distribution Lists is maintained by Collaboration Services for employees and service desk agents. This IT support procedure requesting shared mailboxes and distribution lists.

Owner and Systems

Owner
Collaboration Services
For
employees and service desk agents
Systems
Mail Admin Console, Access Portal

Support Process

  1. Request the list in the Access Portal with an owner.
  2. Define the membership rule.
  3. Confirm delivery with a test message.

Key Facts

  • Distribution lists follow the pattern team-<name>@northwind.local.
  • Every list needs a named owner who approves membership.
  • Shared mailboxes are reviewed annually for active owners.
  • External senders to internal lists are quarantined by default.

Systems Involved

This work touches Mail Admin Console, Access Portal.
