VPN Access and Recovery

Connecting to the corporate VPN and recovering lost access.

Context

VPN Access and Recovery is maintained by Network Services for employees and service desk agents. This IT support procedure connecting to the corporate VPN and recovering lost access.

Owner and Systems

Owner
Network Services
For
employees and service desk agents
Systems
VPN Gateway, Okta SSO, Self-Service Portal

Support Process

  1. Open AnyConnect and connect to vpn.northwind.local.
  2. Approve the MFA push.
  3. If it fails, reinstall the profile from Self-Service.

Key Facts

  • The VPN client is Cisco AnyConnect pointing at vpn.northwind.local.
  • VPN requires SSO plus a push approval on your enrolled MFA device.
  • If the certificate is invalid, reinstall the profile from the Self-Service Portal.
  • VPN is only required for admin networks; most SaaS tools work without it.

Systems Involved

This work touches VPN Gateway, Okta SSO, Self-Service Portal.
