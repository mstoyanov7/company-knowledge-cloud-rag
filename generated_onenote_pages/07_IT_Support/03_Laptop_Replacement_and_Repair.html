Laptop Replacement and Repair

Getting a broken or lost laptop replaced.

Summary

Laptop Replacement and Repair is the IT support procedure maintained by Endpoint Engineering for employees and service desk agents. It covers getting a broken or lost laptop replaced. Standard replacement laptops ship within 2 business days from the local depot.

Key Facts

  • Standard replacement laptops ship within 2 business days from the local depot.
  • A lost laptop must be reported within 1 hour so it can be remotely wiped.
  • All laptops are encrypted, so a lost device does not expose data if reported.
  • Your data is recoverable if you used the synced Documents folder.

Support Process

  1. Report the issue to the Service Desk.
  2. For a lost device, trigger remote wipe immediately.
  3. Collect the replacement and restore from sync.

What To Keep

Keep the ticket ID, requester identity, resolution note with the related ticket or record.
