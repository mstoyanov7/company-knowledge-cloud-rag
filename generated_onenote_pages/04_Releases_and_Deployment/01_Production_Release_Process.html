Production Release Process

How a change goes from merged to deployed in production.

Overview

Production Release Process is the release process maintained by Release Management for release managers, on-call engineers, team leads. It covers how a change goes from merged to deployed in production. Production deploys happen via the CD pipeline after a green staging soak of at least 2 hours.

Ownership

Owner teamRelease Management
Audiencerelease managers, on-call engineers, team leads
SystemsCD Pipeline, Grafana, Slack, Jira

Key Facts

  • Production deploys happen via the CD pipeline after a green staging soak of at least 2 hours.
  • Deploys are blocked during the Friday 16:00 to Monday 09:00 freeze unless it is a hotfix.
  • Every release names a rollback owner before rollout starts.
  • Canary rollout sends 5% of traffic for 15 minutes before full rollout.

Deployment Process

  1. Confirm staging soak passed and approvals are recorded.
  2. Start the canary at 5% and watch error rate and latency.
  3. Promote to 100% if the canary is clean.
  4. Record the release note and monitoring link.
