Feature Flag Management

How features are shipped dark and enabled gradually.

Purpose

Feature Flag Management is maintained by Platform Team for release managers, on-call engineers, team leads. This release process how features are shipped dark and enabled gradually.

Key Facts

  • Flags are managed in the Feature Flag Console and default to off in production.
  • Every flag has an owner and an expiry date; expired flags are reported weekly.
  • Risky flags are enabled by cohort: internal users, then 10%, then 100%.
  • A flag must be removed within 60 days of reaching 100%.

Deployment Process

  1. Create the flag with an owner and expiry.
  2. Enable for internal users first.
  3. Ramp by cohort while watching metrics.
  4. Remove the flag after full rollout.
